Better Carts makes hauling with Carts more pleasant with quality-of-life features, all configurable in-game

[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]Chain Overflow[/b] - when a full Cart picks up an item, it is passed to the next Cart in the chain with a free slot. Nothing is left behind until every chained Cart is full[/*]
[*][b]Bucket Priority[/b] - prefer grabbing an empty Bucket when unloading a Cart[/*]
[*][b]Collect Range[/b] - Carts automatically pick up loose items within a configurable radius (0-10 tiles, default 2). 0 = vanilla[/*]
[*][b]Deposit Range[/b] - Carts deposit matching cargo into Material Storages within range (0-10 tiles, default 2). 0 = vanilla[/*]
[*][b]Connect Range[/b] - free Carts are pulled toward a Cart the player is pulling once in range (0-10 tiles, default 2). 0 = vanilla[/*]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]
All settings (master toggle, per-feature toggles, both ranges) are in-game under Settings -> Mod Settings, or in BepInEx/config/com.beesq.romestead.bettercarts.cfg

[size=4][b]Requirements[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][url=https://www.nexusmods.com/romestead/mods/1]BepinEx 6 For Romestead (Mod Loader)[/url][/*]
[*][url=https://www.nexusmods.com/romestead/mods/8]Mod Settings Menu (Settings Menu)[/url][/*]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Multiplayer[/b][/size]
Server-authoritative: only the host needs the mod, joining players do not need anything installed

[size=4][b]Install[/b][/size]
Extract the zip into your Romestead folder (the dll lands in BepInEx/plugins), then launch the game through Steam

[size=4][b]Notes[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Safe to add or remove at any time, the mod stores nothing in your save[/*]
[*]Deposit takes only matching resources from Cart cargo, Output pads are untouched[/*]
[/list]
